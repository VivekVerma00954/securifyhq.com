About Us

Built to Protect What You've Built

A decade of IT and service industry experience, now focused on one mission: keeping your business safer — without the cost of physical guards.

Our Mission

Filling a gap no one was filling properly

SecurifyHQ exists because we saw a gap that wasn't being properly solved. Across New Zealand, warehouses, construction sites, and retail businesses were experiencing incidents after hours — often only discovering them after the damage was already done.

The options available were limited: expensive physical guards, reactive alarm systems, or passive CCTV that only records events without enabling timely action.

We built something better: AI detects activity on your cameras as it happens, trained operators verify events in real time, and escalation is triggered immediately through a structured response plan that you control.

Our Technology

AI detection. Human decision.

We use Frigate AI — an advanced open-source computer vision system — to detect persons, vehicles, and motion events in real time across all monitored camera feeds. Every AI detection is reviewed by a human operator before any action is taken. False alarms are logged and filtered. Only true events trigger our escalation chain.

Why NZ?

Built for the NZ market. By a NZ company.

SecurifyHQ is a New Zealand-owned and registered company. We understand the NZ business environment, the realities of running sites here, and what local business owners actually need on the ground.

We're not a global monitoring platform operating from overseas — New Zealand is our home market, our focus, and the community we're building for.

We operate under New Zealand law, align with the Privacy Act 2020, and hold ourselves to local standards of accountability, trust, and service. There's genuine pride in building something here in NZ — for NZ businesses — that helps protect the places people work, build, and grow every day.
