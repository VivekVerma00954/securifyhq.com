Live Audio Deterrent

Stop Intruders Before They Act — With a Human Voice

When an alert is verified, your SecurifyHQ operator can broadcast a live, two-way warning directly through a speaker at your site — your operator speaks directly to anyone on site, making clear they are being actively watched, recorded, and that escalation has been activated. Most incidents end here.

🔊

Operator-Triggered Warning

When an incident is verified, your operator activates the speaker and delivers a live verbal warning — not a pre-recorded message. A live human voice — calm, clear, authoritative — telling intruders they are being recorded, their actions are logged, and escalation is now active.

📷

Works With Your Existing Hardware

Many IP cameras already have a built-in speaker. If yours does, we can activate it directly — no extra equipment needed. For sites without speaker-capable cameras, we can recommend and help you source a compatible two-way audio device.

🛠️

We Handle the Setup

Not sure if your cameras support two-way audio? We'll check during onboarding. If additional hardware is needed, we help you select the right device and walk you through connecting it to your system — no on-site technician required in most cases.

⚡

Escalation in Seconds, Not Minutes

The deterrent is triggered the moment verification is complete — before a guard company could ever arrive on scene. Combined with simultaneous notification to you and your pre-authorised contacts, it's the fastest possible response. Where a deter siren is part of your pre-approved escalation plan, it activates automatically at this point too.

💡

How to get this set up

Two-way audio deterrent is available on the Respond plan and above. During onboarding, let us know you'd like it enabled — we'll confirm whether your current cameras support it, recommend hardware if needed, and configure everything remotely. Get started →
