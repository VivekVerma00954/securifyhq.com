Construction Sites

The most targeted sites in New Zealand after hours

Construction sites are a prime target — high-value tools, copper, and plant equipment left unattended overnight and on weekends. A single theft can cost tens of thousands in equipment loss, project delay, and insurance excess.

Most sites have cameras. Very few have anyone actually watching them. SecurifyHQ bridges that gap: we monitor the feed, verify real intrusions, and escalate — so you're alerted before the thieves have loaded the van.

Works with most existing cameras — if your cameras support RTSP streaming, we can connect them. IP cameras, analog cameras with IP NVRs, and most commercial surveillance systems are supported.

Connectivity note: Supported for sites with internet connectivity (4G, Starlink, or wired) and RTSP-capable cameras — whether IP cameras or analog cameras with an IP NVR. We install our VPN router on-site. Not compatible with cloud-only/app-locked camera systems.
