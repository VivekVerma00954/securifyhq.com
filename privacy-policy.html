1. Who We Are

SecurifyHQ Limited ("SecurifyHQ," "we," "us," "our") is a New Zealand registered company (Company Number 9405543) providing remote CCTV monitoring and alert escalation services to businesses in New Zealand.

We are an "agency" as defined by the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ) and are bound by the 13 Information Privacy Principles (IPPs) set out in that Act. This Privacy Policy explains how we comply with those obligations.

This policy applies to our business monitoring services. If you are a residential customer (NRI property monitoring), a separate privacy policy applies.

  • Incident reports and escalation logs

4. Video Footage & Camera Feeds

4.1 Nature of Access

You provide SecurifyHQ with access to your camera feeds for the sole purpose of monitoring and alert verification. We do not continuously record or store your camera feeds. Our AI detection system processes live video streams to identify activity meeting detection thresholds. When activity is detected, a short video clip is captured and retained for operator review.

4.2 Clip Retention

Video clips associated with verified or reviewed alerts are retained for 30 days from the date of the alert, then permanently deleted. Clips are accessible to you during this period via your client portal (where included in your plan) and via the secure link provided in alert notifications.

4.3 Who May Appear in Footage

Your camera feeds may capture images of identifiable individuals — employees, contractors, visitors, trespassers, or members of the public. This footage constitutes personal information under the Privacy Act 2020. You, as the camera operator, are primarily responsible for the lawful collection of this footage. Our processing of it is limited to what is necessary for alert verification and escalation.

4.4 Footage Ownership

You retain ownership of all footage captured by your cameras. SecurifyHQ holds a limited licence to access and process footage solely for service delivery. We will not use your footage for any other purpose, share it with any third party, or retain it beyond the periods stated in this policy.

💡 SecurifyHQ does not make footage of individuals available to any third party except: (a) as part of your escalation plan (e.g. providing a clip reference to police where you have authorised us to contact them); or (b) where required by a valid legal order or court process.

5. Who Can Access Your Information

5.1 Our Authorised Personnel

Your account information, site details, and footage are accessible only to SecurifyHQ's authorised monitoring personnel and management, on a need-to-know basis strictly limited to their role in delivering your service. All personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5.2 Your Escalation Contacts

Alert notifications, including clip links, are sent to the contacts you have designated in your escalation plan. You are responsible for ensuring those contacts understand their obligations regarding any footage or information they receive.

5.3 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information where required by applicable New Zealand law — for example, in response to a valid court order, warrant, or request from a law enforcement agency with lawful authority. We will notify you of such requests where we are legally permitted to do so.

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a sale, merger, or acquisition of SecurifyHQ, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such transfer and your options at that time.

5.5 We Do Not Sell Data

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise commercially exploit your personal information or your footage. Ever.

6. Cross-Border Information Handling

SecurifyHQ's monitoring operations are performed by our authorised personnel. Our monitoring platform is hosted on DigitalOcean cloud infrastructure, with servers located in Singapore and/or other regions. Your account data and alert records may be stored on servers outside New Zealand.

Under Information Privacy Principle 12 (IPP 12) of the Privacy Act 2020, we take reasonable steps to ensure that any transfer of your personal information outside New Zealand is protected by comparable privacy safeguards. Specifically:

  • Our hosting provider (DigitalOcean) operates under internationally recognised security standards and contractual data protection obligations
  • All personnel with access to your data are bound by confidentiality obligations and our internal data handling policies, which require treatment of your information consistent with the IPPs
  • Your video footage is processed in real time for alert detection and is not stored in full — only clips related to alerts are retained, as described in Section 4

By using our services, you acknowledge that your information may be processed and stored outside New Zealand under the safeguards described above, and you consent to this processing as necessary for service delivery.

7. Payment Information

7.1 Direct Debit (GoCardless)

Where you choose to pay by direct debit, your bank account details are collected and processed by GoCardless Ltd, a UK-based payment processor authorised to operate in New Zealand under a Direct Debit Authority. SecurifyHQ does not store your bank account details on our systems. GoCardless processes your payment and remits funds to us. GoCardless's privacy policy and data handling practices apply to the processing of your bank account information.

7.2 Card Payments (Stripe)

Where you choose to pay by credit or debit card, payment is processed by Stripe Inc., a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. SecurifyHQ does not store your card number, expiry date, or CVV on our systems. Stripe's privacy policy applies to the processing of your card information.

A payment processing surcharge is applied to card transactions, representing the exact cost charged to SecurifyHQ by Stripe. This surcharge is disclosed clearly before you confirm payment and appears as a separate line item on your invoice.

7.3 Billing Records

We retain billing and payment records (invoice amounts, dates, payment status) for a minimum of 7 years as required by New Zealand tax law. These records do not include full card or bank account details.

8. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures, including:

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive credentials and account data
  • Encrypted transmission of data between your cameras, our platform, and your devices (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only
  • Secure authentication for staff accessing our monitoring platform
  • Regular security reviews of our platform and infrastructure

No system is completely secure. While we take all reasonable steps, we cannot guarantee that your information will be free from all unauthorised access. In the event of a security breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as soon as practicable in accordance with our obligations under the Privacy Act 2020.

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you or your business
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or outdated personal information

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information where we have no legal obligation to retain it — subject to our retention obligations above

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@securifyhq.com. We will respond within 20 working days as required by the Privacy Act 2020. We may need to verify your identity before processing a request.

14. Your Obligations as a Camera Operator

You are the operator of your CCTV cameras and are responsible for your own compliance with the Privacy Act 2020 in relation to the footage you collect. In particular:

  • Signage: You must display clear signage at your monitored property informing visitors and staff that CCTV monitoring is in operation, who operates it, and how to make enquiries
  • Lawful purpose: Cameras must be used for a lawful purpose — monitoring your own property for security purposes is generally lawful. Cameras must not be positioned to monitor areas where people have a reasonable expectation of privacy (e.g., toilets, changing rooms) without specific lawful authority
  • Access requests: If an individual requests access to footage in which they appear, you are the controller of that footage and responsible for responding to that request
  • Retention: You are responsible for any footage retained on your own NVR or recording system beyond what SecurifyHQ holds

SecurifyHQ can assist you in understanding your privacy obligations as a camera operator but is not able to provide legal advice. For specific legal guidance, consult a New Zealand privacy lawyer or the Office of the Privacy Commissioner (privacy.org.nz).
