NZ Residential

Home monitoring in New Zealand

SecurifyHQ's standard plans are designed for NZ businesses. But if you're a homeowner in New Zealand with cameras already installed and you want a professional monitoring service watching your property — we may be able to help.

Residential sites vary widely — camera placement, internet setup, escalation preferences — so we don't offer a fixed residential plan off the shelf. We assess each site individually and quote accordingly.

Enquire, don't sign up. Don't use the standard business sign-up form for residential monitoring. Reach out via the contact form or email instead and tell us about your setup — we'll come back to you with options and a quote.

Useful to include in your enquiry:

  • Property location and type (house, apartment, lifestyle block)
  • Number of cameras and their make/model if known
  • Whether cameras are RTSP-capable or cloud-only (Ring, Nest, etc.)
  • Your internet connection speed and whether cameras are on a recorder (NVR/DVR)
  • What monitoring outcome you're looking for — alert only, deterrence, or escalation
Contact Us → Email hello@securifyhq.com

AirBnB & Short-Term Rentals

Monitored security for your rental properties

Running a holiday home, bach, or short-term rental? A camera recording to a hard drive isn't monitoring — it's just evidence after the fact. SecurifyHQ can provide active monitoring so someone is actually watching when it matters.

Whether you have one property or manage a portfolio, we can look at what works for your setup. STR monitoring is handled as a custom engagement — no standard plan, we quote per site.

Equally relevant for property managers overseeing residential or mixed-use sites. If you manage properties on behalf of owners and want a monitoring service to sit behind your camera infrastructure, we'd like to hear from you.

Tell us about your situation:

  • Number of properties and their locations
  • Existing camera setup — brand, count, recording device
  • Whether properties are occupied year-round or seasonally
  • What you most need covered — guest check-in/out periods, vacant gaps, or ongoing
  • Any current security provider or guard response in place
