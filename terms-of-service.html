2. What SecurifyHQ Is — and Is Not

⚠️ READ THIS SECTION CAREFULLY. It defines the nature and limits of our service. If you require a licensed security provider, physical response capability, or crime prevention guarantees, SecurifyHQ is not the right service for you.

2.1 What We Are

SecurifyHQ is a remote CCTV monitoring and alert escalation service. We use a combination of artificial intelligence detection and human review to monitor your camera feeds, verify whether detected activity represents a genuine incident, and notify or escalate to your designated contacts based on the instructions you provide during onboarding.

We act as an informed reporting and notification layer between your cameras and your chosen response — ensuring nothing is missed and that the right people are reached as quickly as possible when it matters.

2.2 What We Are Not

SecurifyHQ is not any of the following, and we make no claim to be:

  • A licensed security guard company or private security provider under the Private Security Personnel and Private Investigators Act 2010 (NZ)
  • A physical response or patrol service
  • A crime prevention guarantee
  • A replacement for physical security measures, guards, or emergency services
  • An alarm monitoring company in the traditional licensed sense
  • An authority with any power to enforce, detain, apprehend, or intervene physically

2.3 Our Role

We detect, verify, notify, and escalate — always based on your pre-authorised instructions. We do not take independent action beyond what you have authorised. You remain in control of all decisions, and you remain responsible for all actions taken in response to our notifications.

💡 Think of SecurifyHQ as a highly attentive, always-on alert system that ensures you and your team are notified the moment something real happens — so you can act faster than if cameras were recording and unmonitored.

3. Service Description

3.1 Core Service

Subject to your plan selection and these Terms, SecurifyHQ provides the following:

  • AI-assisted detection: Automated motion, person, and vehicle detection on your connected camera feeds
  • Human verification: Every alert escalated by our AI is reviewed by a trained human operator before you are notified — filtering false alarms and confirming genuine activity
  • Instant notification: Verified alerts are communicated to your designated contacts via call, email, and/or WhatsApp, with a link to view the relevant video clip
  • Escalation assistance: Where pre-authorised, we follow your defined escalation plan — notifying additional contacts, alerting your guard company, or contacting emergency services on your behalf
  • System health monitoring: If your cameras or connected equipment go offline, we detect this and notify you promptly
  • Client portal access: Access to your alert history, reports, and account information, according to your plan tier

3.2 Alert Review Performance

We target alert review within the timeframe associated with your plan. These are operational targets, not contractual guarantees. Actual review time may vary based on alert volume, camera feed quality, connectivity, and staffing. We do not guarantee any specific response time.

3.2.1 Verification Response Times

SecurifyHQ sets internal targets for the time between AI alert receipt and human operator classification of each event. These targets are: Watch plan — approximately 10 minutes; Respond — approximately 7 minutes; Deter — approximately 5 minutes; Shield — approximately 3 minutes. These represent our service commitment and are best-effort targets only. They are not guaranteed response times and do not form part of any service level agreement. Factors including simultaneous alert volume, network conditions, and footage quality may affect actual times. No claim, refund, or liability arises from failure to meet these targets in any individual incident.

3.3 Deterrence Features

Where your site is equipped with compatible IP-based speakers or sirens, we can trigger audio warnings remotely upon verification of a genuine incident, subject to your pre-authorised configuration. This feature is available on Respond plans and above, or as an add-on on Watch plans. SecurifyHQ is not responsible for the effectiveness of deterrence measures — results depend on site-specific conditions outside our control.

3.4 Monitoring Hours

Monitoring hours are determined by your plan:

  • Watch and Respond plans: After-hours monitoring (6:00 PM to 6:00 AM NZST/NZDT, 7 days per week) unless 24/7 monitoring has been added as an upgrade
  • Deter and Shield plans: 24/7 monitoring included

Outside your monitoring hours, camera feeds are not actively watched. Offline alerts remain active at all times regardless of monitoring hours.

3.5 System Compatibility

Our service is compatible with camera systems that support RTSP (Real Time Streaming Protocol) streaming. Compatibility with specific camera models or NVR systems is not guaranteed. It is your responsibility to confirm your system is RTSP-capable prior to onboarding. We will assist in testing connectivity during setup but make no warranty regarding compatibility with every device or configuration.

5. Escalation Authorisation

5.1 Your Escalation Plan

During onboarding, you will define your escalation plan — specifying who should be contacted, in what order, and under what conditions when a verified alert is generated. This plan is recorded in our system and drives all escalation actions we take on your behalf. You can update your escalation plan at any time by contacting us or via the client portal (Deter and Shield plans).

5.2 Escalation Steps

A typical escalation plan may include:

  • Step 1: Notify primary contact (call + message)
  • Step 2: If no response within [your specified time], notify secondary contact
  • Step 3: Notify your guard company or security provider (if contracted and contact provided)
  • Step 4: Contact emergency services (if pre-authorised — see 5.3)

We follow your plan as defined. If a contact is unreachable, we proceed to the next step as instructed. We log all escalation actions with timestamps.

5.3 Police and Emergency Services Authorisation

SecurifyHQ is not an emergency service and is not a licensed security provider. However, any person may report an incident to New Zealand Police. Police pre-authorisation is available on the Shield plan and, where configured, the Deter plan. Where you have explicitly selected police notification as a step in your escalation plan during onboarding, you authorise SecurifyHQ to contact New Zealand Police on your behalf, solely to report a verified incident at your property. In making such a report, our operator will:

  • Identify themselves as calling on behalf of you (the property owner/manager)
  • Confirm the site address and nature of the verified incident
  • Provide any available video evidence reference
  • Not claim authority, make demands, or represent themselves as a security or emergency service

⚠️ Regardless of your escalation plan settings, if our operator observes credible evidence of an immediate threat to a person's life or safety, we will contact emergency services (111). This duty to report a life-threatening incident overrides all other escalation settings.

5.4 No Autonomous Action

SecurifyHQ does not take any independent action beyond what you have authorised. We do not attend sites, deploy personnel, make financial decisions, engage contractors, or take any physical action on your behalf. All escalation steps are taken strictly in accordance with your predefined instructions.

5.5 Limits of Escalation

We cannot guarantee that any escalation action — including notification, siren activation, or police contact — will prevent an incident, result in a response, or reduce loss. Emergency services respond according to their own priorities and procedures, which are outside our control.
